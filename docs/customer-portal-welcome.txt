Subject: Welcome to your Design & Supply Customer Portal

Track your orders, live
------------------------

Your Design & Supply Customer Portal lets you follow every doorset through
our factory in real time - from programming right through to packing - so
you always know where your order is without having to pick up the phone.

Open your portal: https://designandsupply.co.uk/portal


SIGNING IN
Sign in with the email address and password we've set up for you.
- Accounts are created by our team (there's no public sign-up). If you need
  a login, or access for a colleague, just let us know.
- Forgotten your password? Use the "Forgot your password?" link on the
  sign-in page and we'll email you a reset link.


WHAT YOU'LL SEE
Your live and recently completed orders, each broken down by doorset. For
every door you'll see:
- A live production tracker showing the stage it has reached:
  Programming > Punching > Bending > Welding > Buffing > Painting > Packing.
- Your door reference and door type, to match to your records.
- The paint colour(s) with a colour swatch, and the finish.
- The scheduled completion date. Any door on hold is clearly flagged.

The portal shows when the production data was last updated - it refreshes
automatically through each working day.


CHOOSE YOUR EMAILS
On the "Email preferences" page (in the portal) you can switch either email
on or off and pick the time of day it arrives:
- Daily updates summary - a short note listing what changed on your doors
  that day (only sent when something actually changes).
- Daily orders snapshot - a full overview of all your live orders and each
  door's current stage.

You're in full control - turn them both off and just check the portal
whenever suits you.
