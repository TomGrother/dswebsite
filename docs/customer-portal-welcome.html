Track your orders, live

Your Design & Supply Customer Portal lets you follow every doorset through our factory in real time — from programming right through to packing — so you always know where your order is without having to pick up the phone.

Open your portal

Signing in

Go to designandsupply.co.uk/portal and sign in with the email address and password we've set up for you.

  • Accounts are created by our team — there's no public sign-up. If you need a login, or access for a colleague, just let us know.
  • Forgotten your password? Use the "Forgot your password?" link on the sign-in page and we'll email you a reset link.

What you'll see

Your live and recently completed orders, each broken down by doorset. For every door you'll see:

  • A live production tracker showing the stage it has reached: Programming → Punching → Bending → Welding → Buffing → Painting → Packing.
  • Your door reference and door type, so it's easy to match to your records.
  • The paint colour(s) with a colour swatch, and the finish.
  • The scheduled completion date. Any door placed on hold is clearly flagged in orange.

The portal shows when the production data was last updated — it refreshes automatically through each working day.

Choose your emails

Prefer updates to come to you? On the Email preferences page (in the portal) you can switch either email on or off and pick the time of day it arrives:

  • Daily updates summary — a short note listing what changed on your doors that day (only sent when something actually changes).
  • Daily orders snapshot — a full overview of all your live orders and each door's current stage.

You're in full control — turn them both off and just check the portal whenever suits you.
