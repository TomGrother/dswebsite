Who We Are

Specialist Manufacturers of Steel & Slimline Doors for Every Application

With more than 36 years' experience in the UK door industry, Design & Supply manufactures, supplies and installs a complete portfolio of security-rated steel doors, fire-rated doors, flood defence doors, acoustic doors and slimline architectural glazing.

Our steel range includes LPS1175 SR1–SR4 security doorsets, CE-marked fire doors tested to EN 1634-1 with certification up to 4 hours, and flood-resistant doors with watertight performance. For architectural projects that demand slim profiles without compromising safety, we offer Schüco, Jansen and Forster slimline doors with optional glazing, security certification and fire resistance.

Our Story
  • End-to-end service — site survey, CAD design, fabrication, powder coating and nationwide installation by accredited fitters.
  • Fully customisable — hundreds of RAL and BS colour finishes, manual or electronic locking, louvre panels, glazing options and removable transoms.
  • Certified performance — built to LPCB and EN 1634-1 standards, backed by a manufacturer's warranty.
  • Fast lead times — stock personnel doors and Streamline steel doors available for quicker delivery, in as little as 7–10 days.
  • Bespoke solutions — cash rooms, school exits, utility substations, studios: our team designs doors around your requirement.
