FAQs

Streamline Doors, Answered

How quickly can a Streamline door be delivered?

Streamline doorsets are manufactured to a rapid lead time. Contact our team for the current turnaround on your order.

Can Streamline doors be fitted externally?

Yes. Streamline doorsets are suitable for both exterior and interior installation, with open in and open out configurations.

What hardware can be specified?

Project-specific hardware is available, including personnel, panic and access control options.

What customisation options are available?

Side panels, over panels, louvred panels and vision panels can all be specified. Doors are high-quality galvanised steel, with optional stainless-steel upgrades.
