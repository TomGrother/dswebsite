40 Years of Design Precision, Supply Expertise

We are the door specialists. Precision engineering and innovative design across two complete ranges — heavy-duty steel doorsets and sleek slimline architectural glazing.

Steel Range

Steel Doors

For main contractors and trade buyers: LPCB-certified LPS1175 SR1–SR4 security doorsets, CE-marked fire doors with certification up to 4 hours, plus thermal, flood defence, acoustic, stock and Streamline ranges. Manufactured and powder coated in-house, installed nationwide by accredited fitters.

Slimline Range

Slimline Glazed Doors

For architects and specifiers: Schüco, Jansen and Forster profile systems fabricated by our in-house team — glazed doors, screens and façades with security variants certified to LPS1175 and LPS2081, and fire-rated options from EI30 to EI90. Superior quality at affordable prices.
