Slimline Non-Rated FAQs

Which slimline systems are designed for internal use?

Jansen Arte 15 is an internal door system with glazed, flush-fitted or partially flush-fitted options. Jansen Economy is a non-thermally broken system for internal doors, glazing and protection barriers.

Which systems suit external doors, windows and façades?

Janisol is a thermally broken door system for challenging outdoor environments, Jansen Arte 2.0 is a thermally broken window system with narrow profile face widths, and Jansen VISS is a thermally improved façade system for new build and renovation.

Are certified versions of these systems available?

Yes. Janisol is available with LPS1175 and LPS2081 security certification, and our slimline fire range covers ratings from E30 to EI90 across Janisol 2, Janisol C4, Jansen Economy and Janisol VISS Fire.

What finishes are available?

Powder coating is carried out in-house, with a choice of 350+ RAL and BS colours.
