Our fire doors undergo rigorous, independent third-party testing and conform with European Fire Protection Regulations.

FAQs

Common Questions

What fire ratings can your steel doors achieve?

Our fire doors are tested to EN 1634-1 with certification up to 4 hours, built from 1.5mm galvanised steel around a dense mineral core.

Can you integrate access control?

Yes — electric locking and access control hardware options.

Do vision or louvre panels affect the fire rating?

Vision panels and louvre panels are both available, but either may affect the fire rating — our team will advise on a compliant configuration for your project.

Do you deliver and install nationwide?

Yes — UK-wide delivery and installation by accredited fitters.

Can I get full technical specs?

Yes — detailed specifications are provided on request, and data sheets are available in our downloads section.
