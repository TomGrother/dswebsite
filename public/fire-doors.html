Certifications & Ratings

Third-Party Tested, CE Marked

Our fire doors undergo rigorous, independent third-party testing and conform with European Fire Protection Regulations.

  • EN1634-1 tested with certification up to 4 hours.
  • BS 476 Part 22 compliance with ratings up to 6 hours.
  • CE marked, conforming to European Fire Protection Regulations.
  • Secured by Design (SBD) compliant products available.
