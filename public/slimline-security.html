Questions

Slimline Security FAQs

What security certifications do Janisol slimline doors carry?

Janisol slimline glazed doorsets are certified to LPS1175 and LPS2081, with insulated steel profiles designed for high-traffic public spaces and sensitive areas.

Are slimline steel doors available fire rated?

Yes. Our slimline fire rated range includes Janisol 2 (EI30), Janisol C4 (EI60 to EI90), Jansen Economy (E30 to E120) and Janisol VISS Fire (E30 to EI90).

Can Design & Supply provide CAD drawings?

Yes. Our in-house CAD design team produces drawings and technical detailing to support your specification, alongside site surveys and free, no-obligation quotations.

Who fabricates the slimline systems?

Design & Supply fabricates Schüco, Jansen and Forster slimline systems in-house in Merthyr Tydfil, South Wales, with in-house powder coating in 350+ RAL and BS colours and nationwide installation by accredited fitters.
