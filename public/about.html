Who We Are

The Door Specialists

Design & Supply has operated for over 40 years as a trusted UK manufacturer of industrial doors and architectural solutions. We specialise in security-rated steel doors, CE-marked fire doors (EN 1634-1, up to 4 hours), flood-resistant doors, acoustic doors, and the Slimline Schüco, Jansen and Forster range.

Our steel hinged doors feature extended fire ratings and certified LPCB security ratings (LPS1175 SR1–SR4), with customisation options including manual or electronic locking systems, glazed or louvred panels, and hundreds of RAL and BS powder-coat colours.

Many standard sizes are available from stock, with installation in as little as 10 days.

Our Mission

To understand each client's requirement precisely, then deliver the right doorset for it — from free quotation and site survey, through in-house CAD design, manufacture and powder coating, to nationwide installation and aftercare.

Behind that sits a commitment to our people: a skilled Merthyr Tydfil workforce whose craft, built over 40 years, underpins everything we make.
