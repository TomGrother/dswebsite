Who We Are

The Door Specialists

Design & Supply has operated for over 36 years as a trusted UK manufacturer of industrial doors and architectural solutions. We specialise in security-rated steel doors, CE-marked fire doors (FD30–FD240), flood-resistant doors, acoustic doors, and the Slimline Schüco Jansen range.

Our steel hinged doors feature extended fire ratings and certified LPCB security ratings (LPS1175 SR1–SR4), with customisation options including manual or electronic locking systems, glazed or louvred panels, and hundreds of RAL and BS powder-coat colours.

Many standard sizes are available from stock, with installation in as little as 10 days.

Our History

Four Decades of Growth

From leased premises and rented equipment in 1986 to one of the UK's leading steel door manufacturers — our story is one of steady investment in people, testing and certification.

Meet the Team
  • 1986Terry Stares founds D&S from leased WDA premises with rented equipment. Kevin Edwards joins the team.
  • 1987Christopher Weed joins the workforce.
  • 1990Relocation to Merthyr Tydfil on Goat Mill Road; focus shifts to sub-contracting sheet metal work.
  • 1997Move to Pant Industrial Estate — the beginning of the steel door business.
  • 1998Celtic Manor Hotel — substantial order secured.
  • 2003Fire certification obtained for doors.
  • 2004Security certification earned.
  • 2005Fire door range expanded through additional testing investment.
  • 2011Security range expanded with more rigorous testing.
  • 2013Prestigious award won for our Flood Doors.
  • 2015Jodan Contracts Ltd acquired.
  • 2016Full shares acquired from founder Terry Stares.
  • 2017Strategic overhaul of sales, branding and marketing; Slimline business arm launched — 19.2% turnover increase.
  • 2018Turnover up 20.9% as growth continues.
  • 2019Slimline portfolio enhanced through rigorous security testing; 4% turnover increase.
  • 2020Turnover maintained despite the COVID-19 pandemic.
  • 2023New leadership — Scott Davies appointed Sales Director; Damian Regis appointed Operations Director.
