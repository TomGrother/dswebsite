Returns & Refund Policy

We aim to ensure your satisfaction with your purchase. Please review our Returns and Refunds Policy below.

Returns

Returns are accepted only for:

  • Damaged items — contact us within 7 days of receipt to initiate a return.
  • Incorrect orders — we will accept the return and correct the mistake.

To return an item, request a Return Authorisation Number (RAN) by contacting dsl@designandsupply.co.uk. Items must be returned unused, in their original packaging, and in a resaleable condition.

Refunds

Refunds are processed only for approved returns meeting the specified conditions, and are issued within 5 business days to the original payment method.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Items that have been opened, used, or are not in a resaleable condition.
  • Customised or personalised items.

Damaged or Defective Items

Please contact us on the same day of receipt with photographic evidence. We will arrange a refund or replacement according to your preference.

Incorrect Orders

For errors caused by us, please make contact on the same day of receipt so we can arrange the return and ship a corrected order.
