Thermal Rated Steel Doors

U-value performance as low as 1.3 W/m²K. Therma-Line is the latest generation of thermally efficient steel doorsets — engineered for thermal efficiency while maintaining strength, durability and security.

Thermal Performance

Exceed Part L Compliance

Therma-Line doorsets are designed to exceed Part L of the Building Regulations, making them ideal for both new-build and refurbishment projects. Manufactured in the UK for consistent quality.

  • U-value as low as 1.3 W/m²K — reduced heat loss through external openings.
  • High-performance insulated core minimising heat transfer.
  • Vision panels available using thermally rated glass (U-value estimated).
  • Louvre panels available — note these affect U-value performance.
  • Hardware choice affects U-value results — our team will advise.
