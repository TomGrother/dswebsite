Thermal Performance

Exceed Part L Compliance

Therma-Line doorsets are designed to exceed Part L of the Building Regulations, making them ideal for both new-build and refurbishment projects. Manufactured in the UK for consistent quality.

  • U-value as low as 1.3 W/m²K — reduced heat loss through external openings.
  • High-performance insulated core minimising heat transfer.
  • Vision panels — a 200 × 500mm vision panel in thermally rated glass holds the 1.3 W/m²K U-value; other sizes give an estimated U-value.
  • Louvre panels negate U-value performance — any U-value for a louvred door is an estimate only.
  • Hardware choice could affect the U-value, though most options are fine — our team will advise.

FAQs

Thermal Door Questions

What U-value can Therma-Line doorsets achieve?

U-values as low as 1.3 W/m²K, achieved with a high-performance insulated core inside a double-skinned 1.5mm galvanised steel leaf and frame.

Do Therma-Line doors comply with Part L?

Yes. Therma-Line doorsets are designed to exceed Part L of the Building Regulations, suiting both new-build and refurbishment projects.

Do vision or louvre panels change the thermal performance?

A 200 × 500mm vision panel in thermally rated glass holds the 1.3 W/m²K U-value; at any other vision size the U-value becomes an estimate. Louvre panels negate U-value performance, so any U-value quoted for a louvred door is an estimate only. Hardware choice could affect the result, though most options are fine — our team will advise on your configuration.

Are finishes suitable for coastal sites?

Yes. Doors are pre-treated, primed and powder coated in-house with corrosion protection, in over 350 RAL and BS colours plus coastal-grade options.

Do you supply and install nationwide?

Yes. Therma-Line doorsets are manufactured in the UK and installed nationwide by accredited fitters, with site surveys and free, no-obligation quotations available.
