Overview

Tranquil Spaces, Certified Quality

Our acoustic doors combine sound-absorbing properties with high-quality insulation and the security of steel construction. Acoustic seals are customised to the desired dB rating, with insulation options including dense mineral core, plasterboard or e-coustiquilt.

  • 38–51dB estimated sound reduction, adapted to door size and application.
  • Customised acoustic seals aligned with your target dB rating.
  • Single and double rebate frame options.
  • Minimum 5 fixing points per side, plus 2 on the head jamb for double doors.
  • Tailored solutions matched to your space and noise reduction needs.

FAQs

Acoustic Doors, Answered

What sound reduction can an acoustic steel door achieve?

Our acoustic doorsets provide an estimated 38–51dB sound reduction, adapted to door size and application. Acoustic seals are customised to your target dB rating.

How are acoustic steel doors constructed?

Each leaf is double-skinned 1.5mm galvanised steel, 48mm to 55mm thick, built around a vertical anti-twist interlocking welded channel with a dense mineral core. Plasterboard and e-coustiquilt insulation options are available.

What hinges are fitted to acoustic doors?

Pre-treated, protected Grade 304 stainless steel continuous hinges are fitted as standard.

What finishes are available?

Every door is pre-treated, primed and powder coated in-house, with a choice of 350+ RAL and BS colours.
