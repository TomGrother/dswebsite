Questions

Slimline Fire FAQs

What fire ratings are available in the slimline range?

Janisol 2 provides EI30, Janisol C4 covers EI60 to EI90, Jansen Economy spans E30 to E120 for internal use, and Janisol VISS Fire covers E30 to EI90 for façades.

Can fire protection be combined with security?

Yes. Janisol C4 combines security features with EI60 to EI90 fire protection in a single steel system.

Are slimline fire systems suitable for glazed screens as well as doors?

Yes. Janisol 2 and Janisol C4 cover doors and fixed glazing, Jansen Economy suits internal glazing and protection barriers, and Janisol VISS Fire integrates fire-rated glazing into VISS façades.

Can Design & Supply provide CAD drawings?

Yes. Our in-house CAD design team produces drawings and technical detailing to support your specification, alongside site surveys and free, no-obligation quotations.
