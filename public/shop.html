Before You Order

Frequently Asked Questions

What sizes does the Budget Steel Door come in?

Two standard sizes are available: 900mm × 2100mm (frame 890mm × 2094mm) and 1000mm × 2100mm (frame 990mm × 2094mm).

How quickly will my door be dispatched?

Etch prime doors are dispatched the next working day. Powder coated doors take 5 working days plus delivery, with a choice of 50+ colours.

Do you deliver, and can you install the door?

We deliver anywhere in the UK. The door arrives ready to install with hinges, fixings, standard threshold, jacking screws, grommets and brick fixings — and if you would like it fitted, our accredited fitting team can help.
