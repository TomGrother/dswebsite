Flood Defence Steel Doors

Defend, protect, prevail: your shield against floods and intrusions. Engineered for dual protection against both flooding and unauthorised access — with up to 900mm of water protection.

Overview

Dual Protection, Award-Winning Design

Where water threats and security are both critical concerns, our flood defence doors are your first line of defence. Winner of a prestigious industry award in 2013, and backed by over 25 years of experience in steel and security doors.

  • Up to 900mm water protection with steel-lined, high-grade seals preventing water ingress.
  • Security built in — minimum 5 reinforced fixing points per side, plus 2 reinforcing points on the head jamb for double doors.
  • Single and double configurations, with removable centre mullion available.
  • UK-wide delivery and installation.
