Overview

Dual Protection, Award-Winning Design

Where water threats and security are both critical concerns, our flood defence doors are your first line of defence. Winner of a prestigious industry award in 2013, and backed by over 36 years of experience in steel and security doors.

  • Up to 900mm water protection with steel-lined, high-grade seals preventing water ingress.
  • Security built in — minimum 5 reinforced fixing points per side, plus 2 reinforcing points on the head jamb for double doors.
  • Single and double configurations, with removable centre mullion available.
  • UK-wide delivery and installation.

FAQs

Flood Door Questions

How much water protection do your flood doors provide?

Up to 900mm of water protection, achieved with steel-lined, high-grade seals that prevent water ingress.

Do flood doors also act as security doors?

Yes. The doorset is engineered for dual protection, with a minimum of 5 reinforced fixing points per side and 2 additional reinforcing points on the head jamb for double doors.

What is the door constructed from?

A 45mm-thick, 1.5mm galvanised steel leaf with a vertical anti-twist interlocking welded channel core and dense mineral core, hung on a minimum of 4 reinforced Grade 13 stainless steel ball bearing butt hinges.

What configurations are available?

Single and double door configurations, with a removable centre mullion available for wider openings.

Do you deliver and install nationwide?

Yes. Flood defence doors are delivered and installed UK-wide, with site surveys and free, no-obligation quotations available.
