Overview

Certified to LPS1175 1, 2, 3 & 4

Design & Supply manufactures steel hinged security doors providing graded resistance against attack for retail outlets, cash rooms, schools and industrial units. We offer single-leaf and double-leaf doorsets with outward-opening functionality, customisable with manual or electronic locking systems, anti-tamper hinges, vision panels, louvre panels and multiple powder-coat finishes.

FAQs

Security Door Questions

Which LPS1175 security rating do I need?

SR1 resists opportunist attack using basic hand tools. SR2 resists more determined attack with a wider tool set. SR3 resists deliberate forced entry attempts. SR4 is tested against sustained attack and suits cash rooms and critical infrastructure. Our team will advise on the right rating for your site.

Are your security doors independently certified?

Yes. Every door type is tested and certified by the Loss Prevention Certification Board (LPCB) to LPS1175 levels 1, 2, 3 and 4.

What locking systems are available?

Hundreds of manual and electronic options, including multi-point locks, key-operated systems and access-controlled electric locks.

Can security doors be used externally?

Yes. Doorsets suit both internal and external applications, with options for weather-resistant, corrosion-protected powder-coat finishes in over 350 RAL and BS colours, applied in-house.

Do you supply and install nationwide?

Yes. Doors are manufactured in Merthyr Tydfil, South Wales, and installed nationwide by accredited fitters. Site surveys and free, no-obligation quotations are available.
